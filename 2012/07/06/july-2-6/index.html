Blog launch, Twitter site integration

Posted on July 6, 2012

Welcome to my new blog!  This blog is just going to be a weekly/daily journal of sorts, logging all of my discoveries and observations on the Internet and related to development in general.  Of course, expect some anecdotes on gaming and life as well!

Integrated a Twitter feed into the UGM website.  Used a tiny bit of jQuery animate to slide the feed bar out from the left side of the screen.  I also forced the feed to only retrieve tweets if a user actually opens the feed bar (instead of loading the tweets on every page load since the Twitter bar is on every page of the site).  The bar also scrolls vertically with the page no matter its length which works rather nicely.
